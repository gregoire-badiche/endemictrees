Endemic Trees

Let’s grow a better future—one tree at a time!

Welcome to Endemic Trees, a project dedicated to protecting our planet by planting the right trees, in the right places. We work with students and schools across France to raise awareness about deforestation, the loss of biodiversity, and the importance of planting endemic species—trees that naturally grow in our regions.

Why does it matter? Because when we plant trees that belong to our ecosystem, we help bees, birds, insects, and the soil stay healthy. When we plant trees that don’t, we may harm more than we help.

Join us in creating forests that last—not just trees, but whole ecosystems.
